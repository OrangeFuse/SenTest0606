MAT FAQs

Focus the conversation on visibility, variation and scale.

How does Embrace work across multiple schools?

It provides a shared inclusion framework while allowing schools to retain local context and autonomy.

What visibility do we get at trust level?

Trust-wide insight into SEND practice, variation, pressure points and support needs through shared language and consistent data.

Will this increase workload for schools?

The proposition is to standardise and reuse what schools already do, reducing variation and inefficiency.

Can this help reduce risk?

Earlier intervention, clearer evidence and more consistent practice can reduce inspection, tribunal and escalation risks.

Council FAQs

Separate upstream support from EHCP case management.

Is Embrace an EHCP system?

No. It focuses upstream, improving reasonable adjustments and SEND Support quality before statutory thresholds are reached.

How does this help demand management?

By strengthening school-level action, improving evidence and reducing unnecessary escalation into statutory processes.

Does Embrace replace LA professional judgement?

No. It supports shared understanding and better conversations between schools and councils.

Can this integrate with future EHCP workflows?

SENshine positions Embrace as designed to build foundations for future system-wide integration.

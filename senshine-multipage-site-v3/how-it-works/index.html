Workflow

A simple operating model for whole-school inclusion.

1

Teacher gets guidance

Staff access structured, evidence-informed support at the point of need.

2

Actions are captured

Adjustments, observations and reviews become easier to record and reuse.

3

SENCO reviews and leads

The SENCO can see patterns, support quality and where staff need more help.

4

Leaders see the system

School, trust or council leaders get clearer visibility of practice, consistency and pressure.

2. Configure

3. Embed

Support staff usage, SENCO oversight and leadership review without creating another parallel system.

AI use

AI-supported, professionally led.

The safety message should be prominent: AI supports guidance and workflow, while professional judgement, SENCO oversight and organisational accountability remain central.

  • Expert-reviewed logic pathways and recommendations.
  • Ongoing human-in-the-loop review.
  • Feedback into continuous improvement.
  • Research alignment and impact evaluation.

Do not sell AI magic. Sell safer, clearer access to reviewed expertise and better inclusion workflows.

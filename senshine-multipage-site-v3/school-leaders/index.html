Headteacher FAQs

Answer the questions that affect decision quality.

How does Embrace support whole-school inclusion?

It equips every teacher to take practical responsibility for inclusion, while keeping SENCO oversight and leadership visibility in place.

Will Embrace help with inspection readiness?

It is designed to support the inclusive practice, evidence and leadership that inspectors increasingly expect to see.

Does it reduce staff burnout?

It clarifies roles, reduces duplication and helps teachers act earlier, reducing pressure on SENCOs and teaching staff.

How disruptive is implementation?

The product is positioned as fitting into existing workflows rather than replacing them. Data migration needs would be handled with SENshine’s support.

Why SENshine exists

Keep the human story, but place it after the product route.

The original vision page is powerful because it explains why inclusion matters personally and systemically. In this prototype, it supports credibility and mission rather than carrying the first conversion burden.

SENshine’s purpose is to help schools move from isolated expertise to shared responsibility, so that learners with additional needs experience belonging through consistent everyday practice.

The brand should retain the emotional force of “belonging begins here”, while the homepage makes the functional proposition clear more quickly.

Belonging is the brand idea. Embrace is the operating infrastructure that needs to make it practical.

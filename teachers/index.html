What changes

Support arrives before staff confidence runs out.

Teachers are expected to identify need, adapt practice and evidence what happened. Embrace helps make that process clearer and less dependent on waiting for specialist advice.

  • Clearer actions to try in class.
  • Evidence-informed guidance without replacing judgement.
  • Structured records created through everyday support.
  • Better information for SENCO review.

Practice first. Documentation should be the by-product of good support, not a separate burden.
